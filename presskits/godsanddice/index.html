Pitch

Can you best gods at their own game ?

Roll dice against powerfull and dark entities in this tactics games where thinking outside of the box could be the only chance of salvation.

Description

  • A game within a game, in Of Gods and Dice you are playing a board game against dark and powerfull entities.

  • Make Deep strategic decisions in order to overcome the challenges set ahead of you.

  • Dicover die combos that will create new and powerfull die to used in your fight.

  • Roll the dice then assign them to your party, but beware, some die may be powerfull but cursed.
