Pitch

Welcome to recess is a hybrid between a visual novel and a relationship management game with rogue lite elements. Relive your middle school years where every decision affects your path

Description

  • In Welcome to Recess you can explore different storylines throughout multiple timeloops.

  • In each iteration you have the freedom to choose the kind of person you want to be.

  • Which of your classmates will be your become your friend? Which sidequest is worth it ? Pursue popularity or kindness? The choice is yours.
