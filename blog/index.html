Terraflip is in local news !

In September I went to a local game festival in my hometown to present and have people try out my board game Terraflip. It was a great festival with close to 600 visitors over the weekend.

Welcome to Recess won a pitch Jam in Berlin

Another month and another Jam. In September I went to Berlin for a weekend pitch Jam organised by the Digital Gaming Culture Foundation (Stiftung digitale Spielekultur). The objective was to pitch a game that we worked on for a little more than a day on the suject a Teaching Values. We could work alone or as part of a team and joined 3 other participants and we worked together to pitch Welcome to Recess, a game about managing relationships in a schoolyard, discovering the mutiple storylines and playing minigames.
