About me:

I am an astrophysicist by trade but I decided to leave academia to pursue more creative work. As I always loved games and knew how to program I decided to start working in game design.

This led me to participate in a few game jams (see my itch.io profile for some of my games) and develop a board game in collaboration with Castor et Pivert.

I also participated in a pitch jam in October 2023 organised by the Stiftung Digitale Spielekultur. With my team we one the first prize and received some financial help to develop a prototype of a game that tackles the subject of value building during school recess. The game is under developement and more information will come soon.

I am now working full time of my first commercial game. The game will be a tactical RPG using dice, more information can be found here.
